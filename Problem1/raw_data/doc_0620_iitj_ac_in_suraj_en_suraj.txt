Summer Undergraduate Research at Jodhpur (SURAJ) | IIT Jodhpur
Welcome to the Summer Undergraduate Research at Jodhpur (SURAJ), IIT Jodhpur
An opportunity to engage in mentored research with
IIT Jodhpur faculty, explore advanced laboratories, and gain hands-on project,
technical, and research experience.
IIT Jodhpur has a provision for direct admission to the meritorious B.Tech. students from NITs to pursue Ph.D. at IIT Jodhpur. In order to provide a prior exposure of the state-of-the-art research labs of the different academic units at IIT Jodhpur, the institute has launched a summer internship program Summer Undergraduate Research at Jodhpur (SURAJ) from 2025 summer onwards. The following are the main highlights of of the SURAJ.
Opportunity for NIT students to join PhD program at IIT after BTech./B.S./M.Sc.
Offline Mode For Easy Access State-of-the-art research facilities
Poster Session and Oral Presentations and Best Project Certificates
Stipend of Rs.6000/- per month for maximum 2 months
SURAJ offers an opportunity to gain research experience with distinguished faculty from IIT Jodhpur. Applicants are advised to explore the faculty profiles and research areas available on the IIT Jodhpur website before submitting their preferences for
. Applicants can visit the IIT Jodhpur website to explore the faculty s research areas and prioritise mentors according to their interests.
They may contact faculty via email with a well-crafted SoP and resume, clearly demonstrating their willingness to learn and potential contribution to the faculty's research.
