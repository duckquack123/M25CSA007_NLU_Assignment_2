About Recruitment
The Department of Bioscience & Bioengineering invites applications for the positions of Professor, Associate Professor and Assistant Professor from motivated candidates with an exemplary academic and research record in the below areas and their sub-disciplines.
Faculty Members:
Molecular and Cellular Biology
(e.g., Cell Biology, Molecular biology, Microbiology, Immunology, Biochemistry, Physiology, Neuroscience, Neurophysiology, Molecular Biotechnology, Bioenergetics, Biophysics, and laboratory components for these courses);
Systems and Computational Biology
(e.g., Introduction to basic programming languages, Algorithm design and Analysis, Bioinformatics, Biostatistics, Engineering Mathematics, Genomics & Proteomics, Analysis of Microarray and RNA Sequencing Expression Data, and laboratory components for these courses);
Biomaterials and Biomechanics, and
(e.g., Nano-biotechnology, Biomaterials, Bio-Macromolecules & Bioengineering, Biomechanics, Spectroscopic methods, including laboratory components for these courses); and
Biological Processes and Bio-Devices
(e.g., Metabolic Engineering, Bio-process engineering and technology, IPR and Biosafety, Environmental Biotechnology, Enzyme science and technology, Pharmaceutical Biotechnology, Modeling and simulation of bioprocesses, including laboratory components for these courses).
