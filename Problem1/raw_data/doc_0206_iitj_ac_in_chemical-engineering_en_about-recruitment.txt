About Recruitment | Chemical Engineering | IIT Jodhpur
About Recruitment
The Department of Chemical Engineering is seeking applications from interested candidates/ professionals, for the positions of Faculty Members and Staff Members.
Applications are invited from Indian and Foreign Nationals with exceptional academic background, relevant teaching experience, excellent research record and valuable industrial experience for contractual and full-time positions of Professor, Associate Professor and Assistant Professor in the Department of Chemical Engineering to carry out teaching-learning and research in the traditional aspects of Chemical Engineering, emerging technologies, and demand-driven areas such as:
Chemical Reaction Engineering
Transport Phenomena
Chemical Engineering Thermodynamics
Modeling and Simulation
Membrane Technology
Petroleum and Petrochemicals
Molecular Engineering
Polymers and Soft Matter
Biochemical and Biomedical Engineering
Nanomaterials and Nanocomposites
Chemometrics and Data Analytics
AI in Process Engineering
Advanced Process Control
Industry 4.0+ in Chemical Companies
Emerging Technologies
Applicant must have an undergraduate degree in Chemical Engineering or Chemical Technology from a reputed Institute or University along with good academic record throughout from Class X. The Institute is building extensive laboratory facilities with many advanced state-of-the-art equipment to undertake research and development. Further, the Department encourages faculty members to interact with the industry, national and international laboratories to conduct collaborative research and development. For the right candidate: SKY IS THE LIMIT.
