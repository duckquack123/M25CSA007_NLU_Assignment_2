Research Highlights | Materials Engineering | IIT Jodhpur
Research Highlights
Shahzad Ahmed, PhD Research Scholar from the Department of Materials Engineering, IIT Jodhpur, has been honored with the Best Oral Presentation Award at the 2nd International Conference on Materials Science and Engineering, held on October 06 07, 2025
Proud Moment for IIT Jodhpur!
We are delighted to share that Shahzad Ahmed, PhD Research Scholar from the Department of Materials Engineering, Indian Institute of Technology Jodhpur, has been honored with the
Best Oral Presentation Award at the 2nd International Conference on Materials Science and Engineering, held on October 06 07, 2025, at the Millennium Hotel Paris Charles De Gaulle, Paris, France, organized by Prezentis.
Shahzad was invited to present his talk titled:
A One-Step Fabricated ZnO@ITO Electronic-Tongue for Real-Time Spiciness Detection in Chili Samples.
His work presents an innovative real-time electronic-tongue platform for flavor and food-quality monitoring, a promising step toward next-generation smart sensing technologies.
IIT Jodhpur applauds his outstanding achievement
and extends best wishes for his continued contributions to research and innovation.
