Office of IITJ Connect & PRO | IIT Jodhpur
Welcome to the Office of IITJ Connect & PRO
The Office of IITJ Connect & Public Relations Officer (PRO) at the Indian Institute of Technology Jodhpur is committed to building and maintaining strong relationships with various stakeholders, including students, faculty, alumni, industry partners, and the media. Our mission is to enhance the visibility and reputation of IIT Jodhpur through effective communication and engagement strategies.
We oversee the institute s public relations activities, including media relations, social media management, and community outreach. Our team works to promote the achievements, research breakthroughs, and events at IIT Jodhpur, ensuring that the institute s contributions are recognized and celebrated.
IITJ Connect serves as a bridge between the institute and its external stakeholders, facilitating collaborations, partnerships, and engagement opportunities. We organize events, webinars, and networking sessions to foster meaningful connections and to showcase the institute s capabilities.
The Office of IITJ Connect & PRO is also responsible for managing the institute s internal communications, ensuring that faculty, staff, and students are well-informed about important updates, initiatives, and opportunities. We strive to create a cohesive and informed community within the campus.
By leveraging various communication channels, we aim to amplify the impact of IIT Jodhpur s work and to position the institute as a leader in education, research, and innovation.
