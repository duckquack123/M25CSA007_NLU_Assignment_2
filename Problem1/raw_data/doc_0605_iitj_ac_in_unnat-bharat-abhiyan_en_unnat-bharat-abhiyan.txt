Unnat Bharat Abhiyan (UBA) | IIT Jodhpur
Welcome To The Unnat Bharat Abhiyan RCI @ CETSD IIT Jodhpur
Unnat Bharat Abhiyan (UBA) is a flagship initiative of the Ministry of Education (MoE), Government of India, aimed at bringing about transformative changes in the rural development processes. Inspired by the vision of inclusive growth, the program seeks to leverage the knowledge and expertise of higher educational institutions to build a sustainable and inclusive India.
UBA fosters a dynamic relationship between higher educational institutions and rural communities by facilitating the transfer of scientific knowledge and technical support to improve livelihoods and the overall quality of life in villages. The program actively encourages faculty members and students to engage with rural communities, identify development challenges, and devise practical solutions to accelerate sustainable rural progress.
Formally launched on November 11, 2014, by the Honorable President of India, UBA continues to play a pivotal role in strengthening rural India. The Indian Institute of Technology, Delhi (IIT Delhi) serves as the National Coordinating Institute for the program, ensuring effective implementation and nationwide outreach.
Unnat Bharat Abhiyan is inspired by the vision of transformational change in rural development processes by leveraging knowledge institutions to help build the architecture of an Inclusive India and to have a paradigm shift in academic and research programs of the country.
Develop the necessary mechanism and proper coordination among educational institutions, implementation agencies (District administration / Panchayati Raj Institutions), and the grassroots level stakeholders to enable effective intervention at the field level.
Select suitable rural clusters and effectively participate in the holistic development of these clusters using eco-friendly sustainable technologies and local resources, creating sufficient employment opportunities in the process, harnessing multifarious government schemes, customised use of existing technologies, and use of knowledge as per the local needs.
Reorienting the academic curricula and research programs in higher educational institutions to make them more responsive to the needs of the time and to bring in holistic development, and to enrich the knowledge of students about issues of villages/society.

Message From The Director
Prof. Avinash Kumar Agrawal
Director (IIT Jodhpur)
India is a country of villages, and until the comprehensive development of villages is achieved, the overall development of India will remain incomplete. The objective of the Unnat Bharat Abhiyan (UBA) is to advance this very idea, that higher educational institutions dedicate their technical and intellectual resources for the upliftment of rural India.
IIT Jodhpur, as a UBA Regional Coordination Institute (RCI), is working in 14 districts of Rajasthan. Our goal is to link technical activities with the ground-level needs of the villages, so that practical solutions to local problems can be found.
