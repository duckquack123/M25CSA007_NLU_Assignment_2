Workshop on LEAN
The Department of Mathematics is organizing an introductory workshop on the
Lean proof assistant
from 26th to 28th February. The workshop will introduce the participants to theorem proving in Lean, using the Mathlib library, and building definitions in Lean.
No prior experience with Lean is needed
: all faculty, PhD scholars and Masters students in the department are welcome! The workshop will be led by
Dr. T. V. H. Prathamesh
, Assistant Professor of Computer Science, Krea University.
To get a feel for theorem proving in Lean, we will organize mini-seminars from 23rd to 25th February where we will play the
Natural Number Game

Duration: 2 hours

Title: Navigating Lean: Mathlib Library, Projects in Lean, and Other Documentation.

Saturday Morning (28th Feb) in LHC-II (103) from 9:30 PM to 1:00 PM:
