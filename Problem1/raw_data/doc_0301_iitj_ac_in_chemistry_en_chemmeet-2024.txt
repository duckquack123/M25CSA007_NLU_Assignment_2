ChemMeet 2024 | Chemistry | IIT Jodhpur
The Department of Chemistry organized its first in-house symposium, "ChemMeet-2024," on 5th October 2024. The program started at 9 AM in the morning and continued up to 6 PM in the evening. The aim of this one-day symposium was to bring together all the departmental faculties and students and make each stakeholder acquainted with the cutting-edge, fundamental, and interdisciplinary research programs ongoing in the department. This event comprised invited lectures by departmental faculty members and Ph.D. students. In addition, there was a poster session by the students.
