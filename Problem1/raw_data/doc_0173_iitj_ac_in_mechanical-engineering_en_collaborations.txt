Collaborations | Mechanical Engineering | IIT Jodhpur
Faculty Members of the Department of Mechanical Engineering are keen to collaborate with individuals from academia, R&D laboratories and industry in India and abroad, in areas of mutual interest. These collaboration could include:
Teaching of courses,
Organizing short courses,
Authoring of books,
Undertaking research,
Developing technologies.
Some of the current engagements are
Industry and R&D
