Curriculum | Mechanical Engineering | IIT Jodhpur
The Department of Mechanical Engineering offers five-degree programs, namely:
B.Tech. (ME) Program
B.Tech. - M.Tech (ME) (5 years)
M.Tech (Thermofluids Engineering) Program
M.Tech (Advanced Manufacturing and Design) Program
M.Tech-PhD Dual degree in Mechanical Engineering with a specialization in Advanced Manufacturing
M.Tech-PhD Dual degree in Mechanical Engineering with a specialization in Thermofluids
M.Tech-PhD Dual degree in Mechanical Engineering with a specialization in Design Engineering
Ph.D. Program in Mechanical Engineering
