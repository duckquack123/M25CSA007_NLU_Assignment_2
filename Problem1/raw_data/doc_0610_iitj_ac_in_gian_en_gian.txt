Welcome to the Global Initiative for Academic Networks (GIAN) Program
Global Initiative for Academic Networks (GIAN)
in higher education is a program of the Ministry of Human Resource Development (MHRD) to invite accomplished scientists and entrepreneurs living abroad to give short courses at premier Institutes in India.
