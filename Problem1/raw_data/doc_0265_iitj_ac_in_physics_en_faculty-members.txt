Faculty Members | Physics | IIT Jodhpur
Specialization for Positions in Department of Physics
Applications are invited from Indian and Foreign Nationals with exceptional academic record, relevant teaching experience, excellent research record and valuable industrial experience for contractual and full-time positions of Professor, Associate Professor and Assistant Professor in the Department of Physics to carry out teaching-learning and research in the following fields:
Condensed Matter Physics (Theory and Experiments),
High Energy Physics,
Quantum Information & Optics.
The Department also welcomes applicants with background in other relevant fields of Physics. Applicant should preferably have undergraduate degree in Physics or relevant field of Engineering from a reputed Institute or University along with good academic record throughout from Class X. The Institute is building extensive laboratory facilities with many advanced state-of-the-art equipment to undertake research and development. Further, the Department encourages faculty members to interact with the industry and national laboratories to conduct research and development in nationally relevant areas.
The Department offers Masters and Doctoral Degree Programs in Physics. Faculty members are also required to teach courses in various Undergraduate and Masters degree programmes of the Institute.
