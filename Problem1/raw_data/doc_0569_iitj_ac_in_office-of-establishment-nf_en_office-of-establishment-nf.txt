Welcome To Office of Establishment (Non-Faculty)
The Office of Establishment (Non-Faculty) is committed to:
Strengthening institutional governance through adherence to act & statutes, applicable statutory rules, regulations, policies and govt. norms.
Efficient facilitation of staff joining and service management processes.
Accurate maintenance of personnel records, service books, and official documents
Timely processing of salary, allowances, leave, other service related matters
Fair and systematic conduct of the performance evaluation and career progression activities
Promoting capacity building and professional development of staff members
Delivering proactive, efficient, and accountable services for employee welfare, policy facilitation, and contributing to IIT Jodhpur s growth and reputation.
