One Nation One Subscription (An initiative of Govt. of India)
The Prime Minister emphasized the significance of research during his 2022 Independence Day address, coining the phrase "Jai Anusandhan." Aligning with NEP 2020 and the vision of Viksit Bharat@2047, the Government launched the One Nation One Subscription (ONOS) scheme to ensure nationwide access to global scholarly resources for researchers and academic institutions.
