Research Overview
The research areas pursued by faculty members of the Department of Electrical Engineering can be broadly categorized as follows. Please click on the respective groups to know about their research and associated labs.
If you wish to browse Research Areas mapped to the Technology Tracks of the Department, please click on the respective Tracks below to view the research themes, associated research groups, labs and faculty members for each technology track.
Additionally, faculty members from the Department are engaged in challenging interdisciplinary research in collaboration with faculty members across various departments and discplines through the Institute's Interdisciplinary Research Platforms (IDRPs).
