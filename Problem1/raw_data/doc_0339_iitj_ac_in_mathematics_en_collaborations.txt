Collaborations | Mathematics | IIT Jodhpur
Faculty Members of the Department of Mathematics are keen to collaborate with individuals from academia, R&D laboratories and industry in India and abroad, in areas of mutual interest.
These collaboration include
Teaching of courses,
Organising short courses,
Authoring of books,
Undertaking research,
Developing technologies.
Some of the current engagements are with colleagues from the following Institutes/Universities:
IITs, ISI, IISc, IISERs, IMSc, TIFR India
University of British Columbia, Kelowna, BC, Canada
University of Windsor, Canada
Lehigh University, Bethlehem, PA, USA
US Air Force Research Laboratory, Rome, NY, USA
Arizona State University (USA)
University of North Carolina at Charlotte (USA)
Zuse Institute Berlin (Germany)
