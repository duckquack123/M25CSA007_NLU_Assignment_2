Q. Is my CAT score the only or the most important criterion for my candidature for the
While the CAT scores are an important criterion for shortlisting, it is not the sole
determinant of candidature. Other factors such as performance in the personal interview, full-

Q. Is work experience mandatory for the MBA program?
No, work experience is not mandatory. However, full-time work experience carries
weightage in the selection process.
Q. Is the admission process different for the MBA and MBA Technology degrees?
There is no separate admission process for the two degree streams. Once admitted to
the program, students may choose either the MBA or MBA Technology degree after
completing the first year, provided they fulfil the prescribed program requirements.

Students opting for the international dual degree program in the second year are

Q. Will a student opting for the international dual degree be eligible for international
placements? Will the foreign partner institution assist with placements?
Yes, students opting for the international dual degree are eligible to participate in the
placement process of the foreign partner institution, subject to the rules and regulations of
the partner institution and the laws of the respective country. IIT Jodhpur does not impose
any restrictions in this regard.
Q. Is a student opting for the international dual degree program eligible to participate
in campus placements at IIT Jodhpur?
Yes, subject to the rules and conditions specified by the recruiter(s).
Q. Is there any upper limit on the number of students going abroad for the
international dual degree program in a year?
There is no fixed upper limit. However, the number may vary from year to year, and
students are selected primarily based on academic performance at SME and their past
academic record.
Q. As part of the international dual degree program, can a student complete the
specialised master s program at IIT Jodhpur instead of going abroad?
No. The specialised master s programs offered by partner institutions are regular on-
campus degree programs and require students to reside in the country where the partner
institution is located.
Q. Can I add or update information after submitting my application?
No. Once the application has been submitted, no changes can be made.
