Winter School | IIT Jodhpur
Welcome To The Winter School On AI And TCS - 2024
The Department of Computer Science and Engineering and TIH iHub Drishti are jointly organizing the third winter school on Artificial Intelligence and Theoretical Computer Science. The school is being organized as an in-person event.
This year the school will be focused on
Generative AI: December 9-10, 2024
Computation in Social Choice and Economics: December 11-13, 2024
Students who successfully complete the Winter School and obtain a participation certificate will be prioritized for summer/winter internship at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, IIT Jodhpur under the mentorship of Dr. Pallavi Jain or Dr. Pratik Mazumder and/or Dr. Anand Mishra.
December 9-13, 2024
Deadline of Registration:
November 15, 2024
We also have a poster session on
December 12, 2024
focusing on Computation in Social Choice and Economics. Interested participants may share their abstracts in the registration form.
