Research Highlights
Mechanical Design
The Mechanical Design section focuses on cutting-edge research and development in the following research areas:
Vibration and Acoustics Advanced analysis for noise reduction and system stability.
Renewable Energy Development of sustainable energy systems, including solar technologies.
Robotics Design and control of robotic systems for automation and precision tasks, Multibody Dynamics and Control, Automated Visual Inspection, Human-Machine Interface;
Lower limb rehabilitation robots; Medical robotics; Robot manipulator kinematic and dynamic control
Soft Active Materials and Structures Innovative materials with adaptive and responsive properties.
Nonlinear Dynamics Study of complex mechanical behaviors for improved system performance.
Flapping Wing Micro Aerial Vehicles Bio-inspired UAVs for surveillance and remote sensing.
Computational Fracture Mechanics, Computational Solid Mechanics, Adaptive re-meshing strategy, Topology Optimization
Dynamics of Machines and Structures, Flexible Robots, Rotor Dynamics, Nonlinear Oscillations
