Research Highlights | Physics | IIT Jodhpur
Research Highlights
Prof. Ram Prakash from the Department of Physics, IIT Jodhpur has been elected as the President of the Plasma Science Society of India (2026 2028)
Dr. Yogeshwar Nath Mishra has co-invented the World s fastest single-shot wide-field microscope with his collaborators at NASA-Caltech in USA and FAU in Germany. They published their work in the journal Nature Communications.
Mr.Shubham Chamola has received the prestigious Commonwealth Fellowship to work at the University of Cambridge, UK for a duration of one year (Nov 2024-Oct 2025)
Research work of Mr. Rashid Malik Ansari is published in a reputed international journal Small (Wiley)
Jitendra Yadav has received the Galileo Galilei Poster Award at the International Symposium on Beyond Li-ion Batteries held in Padova, Italy (Sept 1-6, 2024).
Ph.D. students, Sipra Mohapatra and Hema Teherpuria, have been awarded the prestigious APS travel grant and Best Poster award for HEMA at CCP2024
Our PhD student Miss Surbhi Ramawat has received the best poster award with a cash prize of 250/- Euro in one of the globally renowned conference on thermoelectric materials.
