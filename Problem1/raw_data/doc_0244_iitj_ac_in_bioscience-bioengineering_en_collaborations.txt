The Department of Bioscience & Bioengineering is also keen on establishing active collaboration with individuals from academia, R&D laboratories and the industry both within India and abroad, in areas of mutual interest. The domains of these collaborations could include:
Teaching of courses,
Organizing Short Courses / Workshops,
Co-authoring of books,
Undertaking Joint Research, and
Developing technologies for diagnostics and treatment by medical professionals.
