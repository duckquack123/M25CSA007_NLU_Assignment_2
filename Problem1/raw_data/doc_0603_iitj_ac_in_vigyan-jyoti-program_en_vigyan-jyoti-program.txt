Vigyan Jyoti Program | IIT Jodhpur
Welcome to the Vigyan Jyoti Program
Vigyan Jyoti program is a new initiative of the Department of Science & Technology (DST), Government of India, to bring gender parity in the different fields of Science & Technology (S&T), where females are under-represented. This program will motivate and provide exposure to girl students (after high school) of nearby government schools towards Science and technology. IIT Jodhpur is one of the Institutes, where this initiative is spearheaded. The activities designed for this program will focus on five aspects, namely Role Models, Society and life, Health, Competence, and Profession and life.
