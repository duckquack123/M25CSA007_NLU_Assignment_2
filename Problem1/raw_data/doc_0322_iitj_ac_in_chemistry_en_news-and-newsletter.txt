Congratulations to Dr Sandip Murarka for receiving the CRSI Bronze Medal
Dr. Sandip Murarka has been conferred the prestigious CRSI Bronze Medal by the Chemical Research Society of India (CRSI) at the recently concluded 36 CRSI National Symposium in Chemistry, held at Dibrugarh University. This distinguished recognition acknowledges his significant contributions to the fields of organic synthesis and catalysis.
IIT Jodhpur congratulates Dr. Murarka on this well-deserved honor and wishes him continued success in his future endeavors.
