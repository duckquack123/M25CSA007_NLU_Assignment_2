Technical Staff Members | Mechanical Engineering | IIT Jodhpur
Technical Staff Members
The Department of Mechanical Engineering has vacancies in regular Staff Members positions at the levels of
(a) Junior Technician,
(c) Junior Technical Superintendent,
(d) Technical Superintendent.
These Staff Members are expected to work closely with Faculty Members and Students alongside other Staff Members to meet the science and technology objectives of the Institute.
Interested persons are invited to apply online.
The Institute has organized its academic activities to be conducted through 11 departments. These are:
Bioscience and Bioengineering
Chemical Engineering
Civil and Infrastructure Engineering
Computer Science & Engineering
Electrical Engineering
Humanities & Social Sciences
Mechanical Engineering
Metallurgical & Materials Engineering
