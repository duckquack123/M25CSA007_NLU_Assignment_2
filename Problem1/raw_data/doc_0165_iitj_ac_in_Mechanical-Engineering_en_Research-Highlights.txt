Research Highlights | Mechanical Engineering | IIT Jodhpur
Research Highlights
The Manufacturing section focuses on innovative research and technological advancements in the following research areas:
Welding Investigating dissimilar material welding, microstructure analysis, and mechanical behaviours of materials, materials processing, nuclear grade materials
Development of welding consumables, structural integrity assessment of dissimilar material joints
Synthesis of Various Nanostructures Developing advanced nanomaterials for enhanced material performance.
Ultrasonic-Assisted Pulse Electroforming Novel fabrication technique for precision manufacturing.
Microsystems Fabrication, Nanotechnology
Clip-on Technology for Legacy Machines Real-time process and product monitoring for manufacturing systems.
Vision-Based Automated Surface Defect Detection AI-driven quality inspection for defect identification.
Vision-Sensor Fusion for Dimensional Measurement High-precision metrology for industrial applications.
Smart Manufacturing, and Product Design and Development
Ultra-precision finishing: Nanolevel surface finishing of wide range of materials using advanced finishing techniques
Additive manufacturing: Laser based additive manufacturing and thermal spray coating has a huge potential in remanufacturing and restoration of high-value dies/molds and aerospace components.
