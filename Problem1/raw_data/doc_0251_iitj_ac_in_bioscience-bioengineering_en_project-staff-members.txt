Project Staff Members | Bioscience & Bioengineering | IIT Jodhpur
Project Staff Members
A number of sponsored research projects are being run by Faculty Members of the Department of Bioscience & Bioengineering. Technical support is required to effectively meet the technical objectives of the project. Towards this end, temporary positions may become available periodically for young scientists and technicians to seek training and engagement with advanced subjects of the ongoing research projects.
Interested persons are invited to apply online for these temporary positions against the advertisement, when announced.
The Institute has organized its academic activities to be conducted through 10 departments. These are:
Bioscience and Bioengineering
Chemical Engineering
Civil and Infrastructure Engineering
Computer Science & Engineering
Electrical Engineering
Mechanical Engineering
Metallurgical & Materials Engineering
