Program Curriculum | School of Management & Entrepreneurship | IIT Jodhpur
Program Curriculum
The curriculum of the flagship two-year MBA program leading to the MBA and the MBA-Technology degrees rests on a common first year curriculum as below:
42 Credits Core Courses + 2 credits Non-graded (NG) Courses
Fundamentals in Accounting
New Venture Planning
Business Economics-I
Business Economics-II
Business Statistics
Digital Enterprise Management
Corporate Finance
Strategic Management
Fundamentals of Marketing
Marketing Frameworks and Practice
Human Behavior and Organization Dynamics
Legal and Taxation Aspects of Business
Operations Research for Managers
Introduction to Operations Management
Business Communication (NG)
Corporate Communication (NG)
Corporate Sector Attachment (4 credit NG)
1-Credit NG Course + 3-Credit MBA Project + 21 Credit Elective Courses
The second-year curriculum offers students choice between two tracks leading to either the MBA or the MBA Technology degree as follows:
Track I Leading to MBA Degree
Track II Leading to MBA-Technology Degree
In both tracks, the students compulsorily complete a 1-credit course on Business Ethics and a 3-credit MBA project.
