Laboratories | Mathematics | IIT Jodhpur
In the learning process, one of the most appealing aspects for students to involve earnestly is by virtue of laboratories. It essentially provides students with hands-on experience of course concepts and with the opportunity to realize the connection between book knowledge and real-world applications. Keeping these points in mind, Department of Mathematics has the following laboratories for teaching purposes:
(1) Programming Techniques Laboratory
This laboratory is for MSc (Mathematics) and MSc-MTech (Mathematics-Data and Computational Sciences) students and is a core component of the curriculum.
(2) Data Analytics Laboratory
This laboratory is for MTech (Data and Computational Sciences) students and is a core component of the curriculum.
For more details of these laboratory, please refer to the MSc and MTech curriculum.
