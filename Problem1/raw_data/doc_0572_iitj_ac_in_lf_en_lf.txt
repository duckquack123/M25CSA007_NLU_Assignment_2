Office of Logistics & Facilities (LF) | IIT Jodhpur
Welcome to the Office of Logistics and Facilities (LF)
The Logistics & Facilities team at IIT Jodhpur is dedicated to ensuring a clean, functional, and well-managed campus environment that supports academic and residential life seamlessly. Our core services include Housekeeping to maintain cleanliness across academic and residential zones, Public Health services such as routine pest control to ensure a hygienic campus, shifting assistance for offices, labs, and hostels, and Event Logistics support to facilitate the smooth execution of institute-level functions, conferences, and gatherings. Through proactive planning and efficient coordination, we strive to provide reliable infrastructure support that enhances the day-to-day experience of students, faculty, staff, and visitors alike.
