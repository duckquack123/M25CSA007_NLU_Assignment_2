Office of Training and Placement | IIT Jodhpur
Welcome to the Office of Training and Placement
The Office of Training and Placement (T&P) at the Indian Institute of Technology Jodhpur is dedicated to bridging the gap between academia and industry, ensuring that our students are well-prepared for successful careers. Our mission is to provide comprehensive career services that support students in achieving their professional goals and to facilitate strong relationships with top-tier employers.
We offer a wide range of services, including career counseling, resume workshops, interview preparation, and skill development programs. Our team works closely with students to enhance their employability and to guide them in making informed career choices.
The T&P office coordinates with various industries to organize campus recruitment drives, internships, and training programs. We are committed to providing students with diverse opportunities that align with their interests and aspirations.
Our office maintains strong connections with a wide network of industry partners, ensuring that our students have access to the latest job openings and industry trends. We also host career fairs, industry talks, and networking events to facilitate interactions between students and potential employers.
We strive to continuously improve our services by incorporating feedback from students and employers, adapting to the evolving job market, and leveraging technology to enhance the placement process.
