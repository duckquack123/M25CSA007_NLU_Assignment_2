Operational Model | Office of Executive Education | IIT Jodhpur
Operational Model
The Office of Executive Education at IIT Jodhpur is built on a robust governance and operational framework that ensures seamless program execution, financial sustainability, and academic excellence. Our model is designed to foster innovation, agility, and collaboration in delivering world-class executive education programs.
Academic Excellence & Curriculum Innovation: Cutting-edge courses designed by expert faculty, integrating industry insights and emerging technologies.
Agile Operational Framework: A dynamic approach to program management, ensuring flexibility and efficiency in execution.
Financial Models
Our executive education programs follow well-defined financial models to support diverse learning pathways:
Independent Executive Education Programs
Certificate Programs, Postgraduate Diploma, and master s Programs operated solely by IIT Jodhpur.
Collaborative Executive Education Programs
Financial model for programs conducted in partnership with external academic or industry collaborators.
Online BSc. Program in Applied AI & Data Science
A dedicated financial framework in collaboration with an external partner ensuring accessibility, scalability, and sustainability for online learners.
