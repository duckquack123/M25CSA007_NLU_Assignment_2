Office of Stores & Purchase | IIT Jodhpur
Welcome To Office of Stores & Purchase
The Office of Stores & Purchase facilitates the following:
Purchase of equipment & stores;
Maintenance of Asset Register; Stock Verification;
Maintenance of Control Store; Maintenance of lists of stockists and dealers for various items of regular purchase;
Liaison with State Purchase Cell and Customs;
Processing of write-off & disposal of unserviceable stores/equipment;
Advising in the framing of rules for Purchase and Maintenance of Stock Registers, Stock Verification, write-off, etc.;
Advising Departments, Centers for Technologies and other academic service facilities with respect to stores & purchase; and
Advertisements on request of Offices of the Institute.
