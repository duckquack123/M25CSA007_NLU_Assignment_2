Faculty Members | Chemical Engineering | IIT Jodhpur
The department is seeking applications from interested candidates, for the positions of Faculty Members and Staff Members.
Applications are invited from Indian and Foreign Nationals with exceptional academic background, relevant teaching experience, excellence research record and valuable industrial experience for contractual and full time positions of Professor, Associate Professor and Assistant professor in the Department of Chemical Engineering to carry-out teaching-learning and research in the following fields:
Chemical Reaction Engineering
Transport Phenomena
Chemical Engineering Thermodynamics
Petroleum and Petrochemicals
Molecular Engineering
Modelling and Simulation
Polymers and Soft Matter
Biochemical and Biomedical Engineering
Nano-matetrials and Nanocomposite Membranes
Chemometrics and Data Analytics
AI in Process Engineering
Applicant must have undergraduate degree in Chemical Engineering or Chemical Technology from a reputed Institute or University along with good academic record throughout from Class X. The Institute is building extensive laboratory facilities with many advanced state-of-the-art equipment to undertake research and development. Further, the Department encourages faculty members to interact with the industry and national laboratories to conduct collaborative research and development.
The Institute has organized its academic activities to be conducted through 11 departments. These are:
Bioscience and Bioengineering
Chemical Engineering
Civil and Infrastructure Engineering
Computer Science & Engineering
Electrical Engineering
Humanities & Social Sciences
Mechanical Engineering
Metallurgical & Materials Engineering
