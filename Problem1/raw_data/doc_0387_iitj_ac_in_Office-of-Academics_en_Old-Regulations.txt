Old Regulations | Office of Academics Affairs | IIT Jodhpur
The Regulations governing four sets of degree Programs offered by the Institute are:
Bachelor of Technology Programs
1. B.Tech. (Bioengineering)
2. B.Tech. (Computer Science & Engineering)
For students who joined in July 2014 and later
3. B.Tech. (Electrical Engineering)
For students who joined before July 2014
4. B.Tech. (Mechanical Engineering)
Master of Science Programs
1. M.Sc. (Chemistry)
For all students
2. M.Sc. (Mathematics)
3. M.Sc. (Physics)
Master of Technology Programs
1. M.Tech. (Bioscience & Bioengineering)
2. M.Tech. (Computer Science & Engineering)
3. M.Tech. (Electrical Engineering)
For students who joined in July 2014 and later
4. M.Tech. (Mechanical Engineering)
For students who joined before July 2014
5. M.Tech. (Metallurgical & Materials Engineering)

1. Ph.D. (Bioscience and Bioengineering)
For students who joined in July 2014 and later
2. Ph.D. (Chemistry)
For students who joined before July 2014
3. Ph.D. (Humanities & Social Sciences)
4. Ph.D. (Mathematics)
5. Ph.D. (Physics)
6. Ph.D. (Computer Science & Engineering)
7. Ph.D. (Electrical Engineering)

9. Ph.D. (Metallurgical & Materials Engineering)
