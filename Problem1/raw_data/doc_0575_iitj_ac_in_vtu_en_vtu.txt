Office of Vocational Training and Upskilling (VTU) | IIT Jodhpur
Welcome to the Office of Vocational Training and Upskilling (VTU)
Future-Ready Skills. Industry-Aligned Training. National Impact.
The Office of Vocational Training & Upskilling (VTU) at IIT Jodhpur is a dedicated institutional initiative focused on delivering high-quality, demand-driven vocational and upskilling programmes aligned with national skill missions, industry requirements, and emerging workforce needs.
In collaboration with government agencies, industry partners, and innovation foundations, VTU bridges the gap between academic excellence and employable skills, ensuring meaningful career pathways for youth, professionals, and public sector personnel.
