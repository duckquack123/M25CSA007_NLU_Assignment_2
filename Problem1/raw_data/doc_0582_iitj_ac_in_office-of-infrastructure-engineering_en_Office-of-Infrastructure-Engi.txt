Office of Infrastructure Engineering | IIT Jodhpur
Welcome To The Office of Infrastructure Engineering
The OIE Department is the engineering works unit of IIT Jodhpur and is responsible for the planning, design, construction of new infrastructure facilities and also managing critical services such as operation, maintenance, upgrades, alterations, etc. This department ensures the successful execution of construction projects and the ongoing maintenance necessary to preserve the integrity and functionality of campus infrastructure. Committed to excellence, safety, and operational efficiency, the OIE Department plays a pivotal role in supporting IIT Jodhpur s infrastructure and its long-term growth.
