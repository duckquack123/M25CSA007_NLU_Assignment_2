A warm welcome to the new Masters and PhD students joining the IIT Jodhpur family! Fiesta 2024 is our special welcome extravaganza designed for our new postgraduate community. This event is a fantastic opportunity for you to connect with fellow researchers, interact with students and your batch mates, and immerse yourselves in the vibrant campus culture. From insightful workshops and cultural performances to engaging sports activities and informative seminars, Fiesta 2024 is the perfect launchpad for your academic journey here. It's a time to build your network, create lasting memories, and embrace the innovative spirit of IIT Jodhpur as you embark on your postgraduate studies.
Fiesta 2023 provided a great opportunity for the new postgraduate batches to showcase their diverse talents and get to know each other in a better way. The event was marked by various engaging activities and competitions, where students actively participated and displayed their creative abilities. It was an enjoyable and enriching experience, as everyone got to interact with their peers and learn more about the vibrant postgraduate culture at the institute. The event was a grand success, helping to create a positive and inclusive environment for all new students.
