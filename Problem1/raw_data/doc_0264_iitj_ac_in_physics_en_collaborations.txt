Collaborations | Physics | IIT Jodhpur
In order to leverage the advantage of complementarity in terms of expertise and infrastructure, it is highly important to establish links with the much bigger world which lies outside the boundaries of the institute. It is therefore very important to tap all possible opportunities of collaborations not only with the academic institutes but also with other equally important stakeholders including funding agencies, R&D organizations and industries. In this pursuit, a number of collaborations have been established both at National as well as at International levels. Some of important collaborations are listed below:
