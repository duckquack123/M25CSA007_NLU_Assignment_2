About SME | School of Management & Entrepreneurship | IIT Jodhpur
The School of Management & Entrepreneurship (SME) at the Indian Institute of Technology Jodhpur
started its operations as a distinct academic unit to integrate management education with technology
and innovation. SME was conceived to meet the emerging need for tech-savvy managers and
entrepreneurial leaders equipped for Industry 4.0 and beyond. It blends core management principles
with cutting-edge technological insights, positioning itself as a technology-driven business school within
an IIT framework.
Today, SME offers postgraduate (MBA / MBA-Technology), Minor Program (for UG students), and
doctoral (PhD) programs tailored to produce future-ready managers and innovators. Its curriculum
emphasizes interdisciplinary learning, live projects, industry engagement, and entrepreneurship
support, encouraging students to work on real-world business and tech challenges. The school also
connects learners with innovation labs and incubators within the IITJ ecosystem, nurturing
entrepreneurial ventures alongside management education.
In the 2025 National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF) Ranking, an initiative by the Ministry of
Education, SME became the youngest B-School to enter the coveted Management Ranking, only within
the 5 years of its inception. The School aims to enter the Top 20 by the year 2030.
