Collaborations | Chemistry | IIT Jodhpur
Faculty Members of the Department of Chemistry are keen to collaborate with individuals from academia, R&D laboratories and industry in India and abroad, in areas of mutual interest. These collaborations include:
Teaching Activities (authoring books and review articles)
Organising National and International Visits for undertaking Short Courses and Research Work
Joint-MOUs for Degree Programs, Research facilities etc.
Undertaking Research Projects and other research activities
Developing technologies through Consultancy Projects with Industries
The Department of Chemistry, IIT Jodhpur is in collaboration with industrial partners as well as top universities in India and internationally.
SOME ACTIVITIES AT A GLANCE
Department of Chemistry, IIT Jodhpur organized GIAN programme with Prof. Mukundan Thelakkat, Univeristy of Bayreuth, Germany
Department of Chemistry and Chemical Engineering, IIT Jodhpur signed LOI for joint research and PhD degree with Kaohsiung Medical University, Taiwan.
Besides the academic program and research work, we also carry out instrumental measurements, chemical analysis, research training and consultancy work for industrial, academic, other research organizations as well as with individuals expressing such an interest.
Our technical staff team is well-trained, experienced, skilled and capable of dealing with diverse analytical and chemical characterization tools. We, therefore, invite all who are interested in our work and potential cooperation to contact us for further information and details.
